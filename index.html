  • The “Mateus Asato” Signature Series
    is handcrafted for those who appreciates
    Neo-Soul as much as the instafamous guitarist from Brazil, Asato. It is now available in two finishes: Gloss Black and Antiqued Shell Pink. Both models are carved from silky-smooth satin finished Roasted Maple.

  • The PRS “Silver Sky” is a vintage-inspired signature guitar designed by the Grammy Award-winning musician John Mayer and Paul Reed Smith. Not only does it create the finest tunes, it is also said that the neck design has a magical touch that just makes you “feel right”.

  • The PRS “Silver Sky” is the result of a close collaboration between Grammy Award-winning musician John Mayer and Paul Reed Smith. The two spent more than two and half years making this vintage-inspired signature guitar. The neck shape was modelled after 1963/1964 vintage instruments.
